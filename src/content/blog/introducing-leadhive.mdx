## How Leadhive Works

Our platform makes finding your perfect agent simple and stress-free:

1. **Share Your Needs** (2 minutes)
   - Tell us about your property
   - Describe your goals and timeline
   - Specify your preferences

2. **Get Matched with Agents**
   - We connect you with top local specialists
   - Receive proposals from qualified agents
   - All agents are pre-screened for experience and performance

3. **Compare and Choose**
   - Review personalized proposals
   - Compare agent experience and strategies
   - Select your perfect match with no pressure
