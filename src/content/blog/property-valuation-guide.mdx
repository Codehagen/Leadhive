Understanding your property's value is crucial when selling. This guide explains the key factors that influence property valuation and how to get an accurate estimate.

## What Affects Property Value?

### Location Factors
- Proximity to amenities
- School zones
- Transport links
- Neighborhood development
- Local market conditions

### Property Characteristics
- Size and layout
- Number of bedrooms/bathrooms
- Property condition
- Recent renovations
- Unique features

## Valuation Methods

### 1. Comparative Market Analysis
- Recent sales of similar properties
- Current market listings
- Price trends in your area

### 2. Capital Value Method
- Land value
- Building value
- Depreciation factors

### 3. Income Method
- Rental potential
- Yield calculations
- Investment return

<Note variant="info">
  Professional valuations consider multiple methods to arrive at an accurate estimate.
</Note>

## Getting a Property Valuation

### Professional Valuation
- Licensed valuers
- Bank valuations
- Real estate agent appraisals

### Online Tools
- Property data websites
- Government resources
- Market trend reports

## Common Valuation Mistakes

Avoid these common pitfalls:
- Emotional pricing
- Ignoring market conditions
- Over-capitalizing on renovations
- Relying on outdated data
- Not considering local factors

<Note variant="warning">
  Be wary of agents who provide unrealistic valuations to win your listing. Always get multiple opinions.
</Note>
