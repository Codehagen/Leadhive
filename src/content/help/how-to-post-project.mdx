Creating a project on Leadhive is the first step to finding the right service provider for your property needs. We'll guide you through the process to ensure you get the best matches.

<Note variant="info">
  Well-detailed projects typically receive faster and more accurate provider matches. Take time to include all relevant information.
</Note>

## Project Basics

Start by providing the essential details:

- Project title and category
- Property location and type
- Timeline and budget range
- Service requirements
- Project scope

## Detailed Description

Help providers understand your needs by including:

- Specific service requirements
- Property details and conditions
- Access information
- Special considerations
- Preferred timeline

## Adding Photos

Include relevant photos to help providers assess your project:

- Current property condition
- Areas needing attention
- Specific problem areas
- Reference images (if applicable)

<Note variant="success">
  Projects with clear photos receive 3x more responses from qualified providers.
</Note>

## Setting Preferences

Specify your preferences to get better matches:

- Service provider requirements
- Budget expectations
- Timeline flexibility
- Communication preferences
- Required certifications

## Review and Submit

Before submitting, double-check:

- All required information is complete
- Photos are clear and relevant
- Budget range is realistic
- Timeline is clearly stated
- Contact details are correct
