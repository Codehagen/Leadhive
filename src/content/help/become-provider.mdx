Joining Leadhive as a service provider opens up new opportunities to grow your business through our platform. We'll guide you through the process of becoming a verified provider and connecting with property owners.

<Note variant="info">
  Leadhive carefully verifies all service providers to maintain high quality standards and build trust with property owners.
</Note>

## Requirements

Before applying, ensure you meet these basic requirements:

- Valid business registration
- Professional experience in your field
- Required licenses and certifications
- Professional liability insurance
- Strong track record of client satisfaction

## Application Process

1. Create your provider account
2. Complete your business profile
3. Submit verification documents
4. Pass our quality review
5. Set up your service offerings

## Provider Benefits

As a Leadhive provider, you'll get access to:

- **Qualified Leads**: Connect with property owners actively seeking your services
- **Smart Matching**: Get matched with projects that fit your expertise
- **Project Management**: Manage all your projects in one place
- **Secure Payments**: Reliable payment processing and tracking
- **Performance Analytics**: Track your success and improve your services

## Setting Up Your Profile

Create a strong profile by:

- Adding detailed service descriptions
- Highlighting your expertise and certifications
- Showcasing past projects
- Including high-quality photos of your work
- Setting your service area and availability

<Note variant="success">
  Providers with complete profiles and quick response times typically receive more project opportunities.
</Note>

## Getting Your First Projects

After verification, you can:

1. Browse available projects in your area
2. Submit proposals to matched projects
3. Communicate with potential clients
4. Schedule on-site assessments
5. Provide detailed quotes

## Best Practices

Follow these tips for success:

- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
- Keep your availability calendar updated
- Maintain clear communication with clients
- Complete projects on schedule
- Request reviews after successful projects
