Join Leadhive's network of professional service providers and connect with property owners and businesses seeking quality services. This guide covers everything you need to know about becoming a Leadhive provider.

<Prerequisites>
Before applying, ensure you have:

- Minimum 2 years of professional experience
- Valid business licenses and insurance
- Professional portfolio or work examples
- Client references
- Flexible availability
</Prerequisites>

## Why Choose Leadhive?

As a Leadhive service provider, you'll benefit from:

1. **Qualified Leads**
   - Pre-screened clients
   - Detailed project briefs
   - Budget-qualified opportunities

2. **Business Growth**
   - Expanded client reach
   - Regular project opportunities
   - Long-term client relationships

3. **Professional Tools**
   - Project management suite
   - Secure payment processing
   - Communication platform
