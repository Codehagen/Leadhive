Leadhive is a modern platform that connects property owners and businesses with qualified service providers. Our intelligent matching system ensures you find the perfect professional for your property needs, whether it's real estate, landscaping, or property management.

## Why Choose Leadhive?

Our platform offers unique advantages for both property owners and service providers:

1. **Smart Matching Technology**
   - AI-powered provider recommendations
   - Location-based matching
   - Expertise-specific filtering

2. **Quality Assurance**
   - Verified service providers
   - Detailed provider profiles
   - Client reviews and ratings

3. **Streamlined Communication**
   - Secure messaging system
   - Document sharing
   - Project tracking

## How It Works

<Prerequisites>
To use Leadhive, you'll need:

- A property or project to work on
- Basic project requirements
- A timeline for completion
</Prerequisites>

### For Property Owners

1. **Share Your Needs**
   - Describe your project
   - Set your timeline
   - Specify your budget range

2. **Review Proposals**
   - Compare qualified providers
   - Review past work examples
   - Check client testimonials

3. **Choose and Collaborate**
   - Select your preferred provider
   - Communicate through our platform
   - Track project progress

### For Service Providers

1. **Join Our Network**
   - Complete verification process
   - Create detailed profile
   - Showcase past work

2. **Get Matched**
   - Receive relevant leads
   - Submit tailored proposals
   - Connect with clients
