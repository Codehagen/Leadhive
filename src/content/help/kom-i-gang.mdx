This guide helps you get started with Leadhive and connect with the perfect industry expert for your needs.

<Prerequisites>
Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A clear understanding of your project needs
- Basic information about your requirements
- An idea of your timeline
</Prerequisites>

## 1. Share Your Needs

To find the best expert for you, we need some details:

- **Service Type**: Real Estate, Landscaping, or other services
- **Location**: Your address and postal code
- **Timeline**: When you need the service
- **Specific Requirements**: Any special needs or preferences

<Note variant="info">
  It takes just 2 minutes to share your needs, and our service is completely free with no obligations.
</Note>

## 2. Get Expert Proposals

After sharing your needs, you'll:

1. Receive proposals from qualified professionals
2. View detailed expert profiles
3. Compare rates and service plans

## 3. Choose Your Expert

To select the best professional, consider:

1. **Compare Proposals**
   - Review service rates
   - Evaluate proposed strategies
   - Check past results
   - Read client testimonials

2. **Assess Expertise**
   - Local knowledge
   - Experience with similar projects
   - Communication style
   - Availability

<Note variant="warning">
  Take time to review all proposals before making your decision.
</Note>

## 4. Start Your Project

Once you've chosen your expert:
- Review and sign agreements
- Plan project timeline
- Discuss strategy and approach
- Set up milestones

## 5. Track Progress

1. **Project Management**
   - Regular updates
   - Progress tracking
   - Communication hub
   - Performance monitoring

2. **Project Completion**
   - Quality assurance
   - Final review
   - Feedback collection
   - Future planning

In your Leadhive dashboard you can:
- Track project status
- Communicate with experts
- View progress updates
- Access analytics and insights

  Our customer service team is available via chat and phone if you need help!

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **How long does it take to receive expert proposals?**
   - Usually 24-48 hours
   - Faster in major areas
   - Depends on service type

2. **Is the service really free?**
   - Yes, Leadhive is completely free for clients
   - You only pay standard industry rates to your chosen expert

3. **Can I switch experts?**
   - Yes, if you're not satisfied we can help you find a new expert
